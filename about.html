About Me

I'm a third-year student at California State University Fullerton.
At first, I wasn’t sure which path I wanted to take, but after spending some time learning on my own, I discovered a passion for web development.

For me, school has always been a challenge. I initially planned to major in business, but when things didn’t work out, I decided to leave and focus on working until 2021. That year, I returned to school with a fresh start. Switching my major to computer science has given me more motivation than ever to finish my degree.

When I'm not busy with school, I enjoy a variety of hobbies. I'm an avid gamer, playing everything from FPS and RPGs to more casual games. Right now, my main game is MapleStory. Outside of gaming, I enjoy photography, experimenting with both digital and film, including small and medium formats. When I'm not gaming or behind a camera, I like to watch web development tutorials or work on some side projects to expand my skills.
